MOMO


Description

Momos are soft, steamed dumplings popular across Nepal, Tibet, and India. They have a thin dough shell filled with vegetables or meat and are served with a spicy chutney. They’re light, delicious, and make a perfect snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Water (for kneading)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • ½ cup chopped carrots
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to knead a soft dough; rest it for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and sauté onions, cabbage, carrots, ginger-garlic, pepper, and salt.
  3. Roll small dough balls into thin circles.
  4. Place filling in the center and fold into momo shapes.
  5. Steam for 10–12 minutes until they look shiny.
  6. Serve with spicy momo chutney.
