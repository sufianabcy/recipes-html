Chole


Description

Chole is a popular North Indian curry made with chickpeas simmered in a spicy, tangy tomato-onion gravy. It’s rich, flavorful, and perfect with bhature, puri, or steamed rice. This dish is known for its deep color and aromatic spice blend that gives it a warm and comforting taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2–3 green chilies
  • 2 tsp chole masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Pressure-cook the soaked chickpeas until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onions, and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and spices; cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add boiled chickpeas and some of the cooking water.
  6. Simmer 10–15 minutes until thick.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
